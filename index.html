Our aim is to support individuals with their new journey from the initial stage of accepting Islam, known as the Testimony of Faith 'Shahadah'.

We provide educational, spiritual, and social mentoring to develop an active and confident new member of the Islamic community.

Our education department has classes to best fit your learning outcomes from fundamentals to advanced learning. We offer:

  • Primary Madani Qaidah lessons (learn to read the Quran)
  • Lessons on ritual purity (Wudu)
  • Guidance on the 5 daily prayers (Salah)
